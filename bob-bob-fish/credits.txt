## BOB BOB FISH ##

Developed by Liam Chessell (liamchessell.com)

Developed for #1GAM April 2015 (www.onegameamonth.com)

Developed using the following:

- pixi.js (Rendering)
- keydrown.js (Keyboard input)
- howler.js (Audio handling)
- Photoshop CS6 (Image editing)
- www.bfxr.net (Game over sound effect)

With assets taken from the following:

- Youtube creator audio library (background music)
- cd.textfiles.com ("pop" sound effect)

If you like the game please follow me on twitter! (@DukeToblerone)
