I am an active member of the #1GAM (www.onegameamonth.com) community, and have released several projects since January 2015. I have moved from creating games in Game Maker Studio to building games in HTML5 with the use of javascript frameworks such as Phaser and PIXI. I have also been exposed to other software during my membership, including Tiled, TexturePacker, Sublime Text 2 among others.

I have released two games to iTunes prior to 2015, which were developed in Xcode using Objective-C and also used the Cocos2D framework. At some point I wish to come back to this to develop mobile as well as web games.

HELI{O}MANCERS (#1GAM July 2015)

HELI{O}MANCERS was created using HTML5 and Phaser.js for #1GAM July 2015, and is now released and available to play through your browser.

Click here to play the game on itch.io!

HELI{O}MANCERS is a platform shooter. You take the role of a Heliomancer who uses light magic to shoot orbs of light at enemies. Your job is to clear the dungeon of monsters so that your village can reclaim the power generator that lies wthin. The game consists of a one level demo with the intention of making a bigger game in the future.

Happy Glider (#1GAM June 2015)

Happy Glider was created using HTML5 and Phaser.js for #1GAM June 2015, and is available to play through your browser.

Click here to play the game on itch.io!

Happy Glider is an endless runner where you fly a plane through increasingly difficult patterns of destructive mines! Catch balloons to speed yourself up temporarily, but watch out, as this makes you more susceptible to crash and burn! How far can you go?

Charming Chameleon (#1GAM March 2015)

Charming Chameleon was created in Game Maker Studio for #1GAM March 2015, and is available to play on Windows.

This game has been reviewed on youtube by twitter user @Jupiter_Hadley, who has also featured it in an article of her favourite games out of all the entries for #1GAM March 2015!

Click here to download the game from itch.io!

Click here to watch @Jupiter_Hadley's video!

Click here to read @Jupiter_Hadley's article!

You play as the charming chameleon in a quest to gather as many butterflies for his female companion in order to impress her. Blend into different colours in order to scare enemies, hide from them, and attract butterflies to the level exit!
