(WIP) Strategy Game

Created for #1GAM September 2015

This game is still in a concept state and by no means complete!

Two players can play locally on the same PC/Laptop

Move a unit by selecting it and clicking on the movable blue squares that appear. Pressing [Enter] will end your turn.

If you're in range with an enemy you can click on a unit on a red square to fight!

Sandy terrain is slower than grassy terrain to move on.

Last player standing wins!
